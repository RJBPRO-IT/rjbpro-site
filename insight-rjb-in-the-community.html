RJB Builds with Habitat for Humanity

April 4, 2025 · RJB Contracting

At RJB Contracting, our commitment to community goes beyond the job site. Giving back is part of building something bigger — and this April, we had the chance to do exactly that.

On Friday, April 4th, members of the RJB team joined Habitat for Humanity of Chester County for a volunteer day focused on making safe, affordable housing a reality. We spent the day insulating two homes that will soon belong to local families. From unloading truckloads of donated insulation to making sure every wall and corner was properly covered, the team brought real hands-on help to the project.

It reflects how we think about our work: we don't just build structures, we help build stronger communities.

Supporting Hops for Homes

We're also proud to sponsor Hops for Homes, Habitat's annual beer festival. This year's event is Saturday, June 14th at a new location, the Chester County Art Association — a day of great brews, good food, and community spirit, all in support of Habitat's mission to provide secure, affordable homes for families across Chester County.

  • Date: Saturday, June 14th
  • Location: Chester County Art Association
  • Tickets: Early bird tickets on sale Monday, April 14th

Interested in getting involved? Habitat is looking for event sponsors, alcohol vendors, craft vendors, and food trucks. For details, contact Christy Kane, Director of Development and Marketing, at christy@hfhcc.org.

We're honored to play a small role in helping families achieve the dream of homeownership, and we look forward to continuing this partnership for years to come. To learn more, visit Hops for Homes Event Details.
