Building Together: RJB's Second Year with Habitat for Humanity

April 17, 2026 · RJB Contracting

At RJB Contracting, our commitment to community extends beyond the job site. We believe building goes further than structures — it's about creating stronger communities and making a lasting difference where we live and work.

This spring, members of the RJB team again partnered with Habitat for Humanity of Chester County for our second annual volunteer build day — continuing a partnership that means a great deal to our team.

Returning to the same neighborhood where we volunteered last year made it especially meaningful. Last year, our team spent the day unloading donated insulation and insulating homes that would soon belong to local families. This year, we got to see those same homes completed, occupied, and full of life.

Seeing families moved in and the neighborhood taking shape was a reminder that small contributions add up to something much bigger.

For our second build day, the RJB team painted trim throughout an additional section of homes heading to market, next to the ones we helped with last year. Knowing those homes will soon welcome new families made every brush stroke worth it.

Construction is what we do every day, but days like these remind us why. Supporting organizations like Habitat for Humanity lets us put our skills and time toward safe, affordable housing — and toward stronger communities for years to come.

Our support goes beyond the build days, too. We're proud to back Habitat's work throughout the year and to keep helping their mission however we can.

We're grateful to Habitat for Humanity of Chester County for letting us play a small part in this work, and we look forward to continuing the partnership for years to come.

Because building communities is about more than what we construct. It's about the people who call those places home.
