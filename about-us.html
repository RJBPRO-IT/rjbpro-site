Our Story

From one trade to one accountable partner.

Ron Bacskai founded RJB Contracting in 1992 outside Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, as a specialized commercial coatings contractor. From day one, the work was grounded in something simple: show up, do it right, and stand behind it. That standard hasn't changed.

As national retail brands began trusting RJB with more of their facilities, the scope grew. Clients didn't want multiple contractors. They wanted one accountable partner who could handle everything from paint to finishes to full interior construction. RJB answered that demand trade by trade — drywall, carpentry, fixturing — until it became the general contractor it is today.

Headquartered in Spring City, PA, RJB now operates across 48 states, supporting the most demanding retail programs in the country. The same family that started with a single trade now runs one of the most trusted national commercial contracting companies in the business.

How We Work

Four decades of commercial construction have taught us what separates contractors who show up from contractors who deliver. It comes down to four things we refuse to compromise on.

Accountability
One point of contact. One team on-site. If something isn't right, we own it — no finger-pointing, no passing the buck to a subcontractor.

Working in live retail and industrial environments demands a culture of safety. Every crew member is trained, accountable, and empowered to stop work if something isn't right.
Family-Owned

Building for the next 40 years.

When Ron Bacskai started RJB in 1992, he wasn't thinking about exit strategies. He was thinking about the next job. That long-term mindset is built into every decision at RJB — from how we invest in our workforce to how we choose the clients we partner with.

Today, the second generation of the Bacskai family is part of the business, bringing fresh energy to the same foundation. Family ownership means we answer to our clients and our crew — not to a corporate mandate or a quarterly earnings call. That freedom lets us do the right thing, every time.
